# Polar as Merchant of Record

AgentKogei will use Polar as the MVP billing provider and Merchant of Record for the $99 annual Premium Access subscription, using the existing Better Auth integration for checkout, customer state, portal access, and verified billing events. Polar will be the billing source of truth and handle international sales-tax obligations, while AgentKogei will own Pack Credentials, Project Licenses, and authenticated registry delivery rather than treating Polar as the pack host. AgentKogei will offer no voluntary refunds; refunds required by law, card-network rules, or issued by Polar remain exceptions and terminate Premium Access and Project Licenses from the refunded period. We accept Polar's higher transaction fee in exchange for materially lower tax and billing operational burden during validation.
